Biography

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is a former international cricketer from India, widely recongnized as one of the greatest batsmen in the history if cricket. He was born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India.

Achievements

Cricket Records

Sachin Tendulkar holds numerous cricket records, including being the highest runs-scorer in both Test and One-Day International (ODI) cricket. He achieved several milestones throughout his illustrious career.

International Honors

He received awards and honors, including the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 2014. His contribution to Indian cricket is unparallerled.

Records

Sachin tendulkar holds records for the most runs in international cricket, most centuries in both Test and ODI formats, and many more. His records are a testament to his exceptional skill and longevity in the sport.
