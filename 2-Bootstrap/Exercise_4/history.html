History of Mumbai City

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, has a rich history that dates back centuries. Here are key milestones that shaped the city:

Ancient Period

Mumbai has evidence of human habitation dating back to the Stone Age. The city was a collection of islands inhabited by the Koli fishing community.

Colonial Era

The Portuguese, followed by the British, had a significant impact on Mumbai. The British East India Company developed the city as a trading hub, and Mumbai quickly grew in importance.

Independence and Post-Independence Mumbai

Mumbai played a crucial role in India's struggle for independence. After gaining freedom in 1947, Mumbai became the capital of the newly formed state of Maharashtra in 1960

Economic Hub

Over the years, Mumbai evolved into the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of India. the city's skyline is dotted with skycrappers, and it continues to be a melting and diverse cultures and communities.
