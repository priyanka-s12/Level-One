Interesting Facts About Mumbai City

Dabbawalas

Mumbai's dabbawalas are known for their effective lunchbox delivery system, with a Six Sigma certification for accuracy.

Bollywood

Mumbai is the heart of the Indian film industry, commonly known as Bollywood, producing numerous films each year.

Elephanta Caves

The Elephanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, feature rock-out caves dedicated to Hindu, and Buddhist deities.
