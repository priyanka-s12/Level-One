Mumbai City Cuisine

Mumbai is a culinary paradise with a diverse range of flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and food experiences in the city:

Pav Bhaji

A popular street dish consisting pf a spicy vegetable curry (bhaji) served with soft bread rolls (pav).

Bhel Puri

A delightful combination of puffed rice, sev, chutaneys, and various ingredients, creating a tasty snack.

Vada Pav

Known as the "Indian Burger", vada pav consists of a soicy potato fritter (vada) placed inside a pav.
