Early Life

Bill Gates, born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington, is an American business magnet, software developer, and philanthropist. He showed an early interest in computers and programming, creating his first computer program at the age of 13.

Co-founding Microsoft

In 1975, along with his childhood friend Paul Allen, Gates co-founded Microsoft, a company that would play a significant role in the personal computer revolution. Microsoft's operating system, MS-DOS, bacame the foundation for their success.

Philanthropy

In 2000, Bill Gates and his then ex-wife Melenda established the Bill and Melenda Gates Foundation, one of the world's largest private charitable foundations. The foundation focuses on global health, education, and poverty alleviation, contributing billions to various causes.

Global Impact

Bill Gates has had a profound impact on technology, business, and philanthropy. His vision, leadership, and contributions continue to shape the modern world, making him one of the most influential figures in the industry.
